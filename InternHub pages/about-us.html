About Us


Our mission

Students often struggle to find suitable internships or training with companies because existing university systems offer limited opportunities and don’t cover all specialties. This gap makes it hard for many students to get real-world experience at the right time in their studies.

What we do

This platform helps students discover internships and training opportunities that align with their skills and the subjects they’ve completed. By focusing on what students actually know and can do, the platform surfaces relevant, practical opportunities instead of generic listings.

Why it’s better

Unlike the university training system, which is limited in scope and availability, this platform expands access across specialties and organizations. It prioritizes fit by matching opportunities to each student’s skills and coursework, helping students find roles where they can contribute, learn, and grow faster.

    How it works

  • Create a profile with your skills and completed subjects.
  • Browse internships and training filtered to your background.
  • Save, apply, and track your applications in one place.
